# W8-EVAL-01 Human Review Rubric

Review chart-fact correctness, answer relevance, evidence grounding, citation validity, safety/tone, and critical defects. Reviewers must be blinded to automatic scores initially. Triggered items require two reviewers and adjudication. Critical defects include wrong chart facts, material evidence contradiction, fabricated citations, cross-chart leakage, harmful deterministic advice, or reversal of the gold conclusion.
